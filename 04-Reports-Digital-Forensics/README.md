# 🕵️ Digital Forensics

## Overview

As part of the CySecK Cybersecurity Finishing School, I gained practical exposure to digital forensic investigation methodologies through hands-on laboratory exercises and guided investigations. The training focused on the systematic acquisition, preservation, analysis, and reporting of digital evidence using industry-recognized forensic tools.

The objective was to understand how digital evidence is collected, analyzed, and documented while maintaining the integrity of the investigation process.

---

# 🎯 Learning Objectives

During this module, I learned to:

- Acquire and preserve digital evidence
- Perform forensic disk imaging
- Recover deleted files
- Analyze metadata and file systems
- Investigate Windows-based systems
- Maintain evidence integrity and chain of custody
- Generate structured forensic investigation reports

---

# 🔬 Practical Investigations

Hands-on activities included:

- Digital evidence acquisition
- Disk imaging

- Registry and system artifact examination
