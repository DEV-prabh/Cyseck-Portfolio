# 🏛️ IISc Bengaluru

## Overview

As part of the **CySecK Cybersecurity Finishing School**, I had the opportunity to attend the **Annual Cybersecurity Conference at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bengaluru**. The event brought together cybersecurity researchers, industry experts, entrepreneurs, startups, and students to discuss emerging security challenges, research, and innovations.

---

# 🌟 Highlights

- Participated in the **IISc Cybersecurity Capture The Flag (CTF)** competition.
- Interacted with **cybersecurity researchers, industry experts, startup founders, and entrepreneurs**.
- Attended technical talks and explored current cybersecurity research and emerging technologies.
- Networked with professionals from academia, industry, and the startup ecosystem.
- Received an opportunity to participate in the **Cloudflare recruitment process**, successfully clearing the initial screening rounds conducted during the event.

---

# 📈 Key Takeaways

- Exposure to cutting-edge cybersecurity research and innovation.
- Practical experience through competitive CTF challenges.
- Valuable networking with professionals from academia and industry.
- Insights into cybersecurity career opportunities and industry expectations.
